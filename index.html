What is the hat puller? Have you ever entered somthing like a raffle where the winner was selected randomly by, "pulling from a hat"? Thats what this app does, papercut free. All you have to do is input what you want on your card(s), this can be whatever you want! Can't deside where you want to eat? make a card for all your favorite grub dealers. Debating on who gets the last cupcake? Make a card for all of your fellow mini cake enthusiast's! After you fill out a card just click the "Add Card" button. Your new card will then displayed on the page. You can add as many cards as your heart desires. Once you're happy with the card's you made, all you have to do is click the "Pick One" button! The winning card then appears! Have fun with it!
